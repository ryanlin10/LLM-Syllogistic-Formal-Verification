=== Experiment 2: Positional Permutation ===

N examples: 100
Original accuracy (original premise order): 0.910 (91/100)
Permuted accuracy (shuffled premise order):  0.610 (61/100)
Accuracy delta (orig - perm):                +0.300
Stability (same conclusion output):           0.630 (63/100)
Consistency (same correctness pre/post):      0.660

Outcome breakdown:
  both_correct                  : 0.590
  neither_correct               : 0.070
  only_original_correct         : 0.320
  only_permuted_correct         : 0.020

Interpretation:
  High stability (~1.0): model output is order-invariant (robust)
  Low stability: model is sensitive to premise order (positional bias)
